Our main Fields of Practice

For Businesses

We take care of everything, from setting up your global team in the U.S. to ensuring legal compliance. More than just paperwork, our goal is to help your business thrive in the corporate world.

For Investors

Considering expanding your business to the U.S.? Look no further. Our investor immigration services offer comprehensive assistance, from obtaining investor visas to managing international investments and establishing your American branch.

For Families

We're fully committed to reuniting you with your loved ones in the U.S., stress-free. Navigating visas? Check. Legal residency? Check. The joy of having your family together under one roof, just like you've always dreamed? Triple check.

For Victims of Crimes

Our team provides personalized, empathetic, and confidential support, guiding you through every visa process step while prioritizing your safety and rights. Remember, you're not alone in this journey - we're with you every step of the way.

Serving Immigrants, Inc. is a dynamic full-service law firm specializing in assisting immigrants and their loved ones with a wide range of legal situations. Led by attorney Magdalena Cuprys, a refugee turned U.S. citizen with personal immigration experience, our firm is dedicated to helping immigrants achieve their American dream. Beyond immigration-related cases, we also handle criminal defense, personal injury law, estate planning, family law, and more, with a network of attorneys nationwide. With offices in Miami, Clewiston, and Florida, we provide personalized legal representation tailored to our clients' needs, striving to deliver five-star customer service akin to the finest hotels. Our team has decades of combined experience in immigration law, helping countless clients navigate various immigration matters, from citizenship to business immigration, family immigration, asylum, and more. We understand the integral role immigration plays in enriching the cultural tapestry and economic growth of the United States, and we are committed to providing support and creative solutions to individuals, families, professionals, and businesses nationwide, regardless of the complexity of their immigration needs. Led by attorney Magdalena Cuprys, our firm effectively serves clients across the United States from our offices in Coral Gables, FL, and Clewiston, FL.
